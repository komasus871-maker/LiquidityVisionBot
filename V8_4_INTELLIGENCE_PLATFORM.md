# LiquidityVisionBot v8.4.0 — Intelligence Platform

Major release built on v8.0.3 lifecycle integrity.

## New public intelligence modules

- `/performance` — expectancy, profit factor, net R, average win/loss, hold time, streaks and best markets.
- `/portfolio` — portfolio heat, directional concentration, symbol concentration, estimated open R and active-position dashboard.
- `/dna` — learned strengths and weaknesses across symbol, timeframe and direction cohorts.
- `/insights` — compact decision brief combining system edge, Trade DNA and portfolio risk.

## Premium foundation

The free product receives complete core analytics. Premium is positioned for deeper session DNA, setup fingerprints, similarity cohorts, exports and personalized risk rules instead of artificial degradation of the free tier.

## Compatibility

No destructive database migration is required. All analytics operate on existing signal lifecycle data.
