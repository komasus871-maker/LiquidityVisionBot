# Stage 5 — Execution Intelligence

- Separate Direction Score, Entry Quality, Risk Quality, and Execution Readiness.
- Blocking entry location can no longer remain READY.
- New execution states: READY, WAIT FOR PULLBACK, WAIT FOR TRIGGER, REVERSAL WATCH, WATCHLIST.
- Preferred entry zones derived from dealing range and ATR.
- Scanner 2.0 categories: Ready Now, Pullback Opportunities, Confirmation Watch, Reversal Watch, Best Shorts.
- Market Intelligence adds average readiness and recovery/expansion regimes.
- Signal lifecycle separates WATCHING from ACTIVE, TP1, TP2, TP3, STOP.
- Journal and Profile separate watched setups, active trades, and closed trades.
