# Liquidity Vision v6.0 — Phase 1: Intelligence Core

Implemented in this release:

- Dynamic Confidence recalculated on every active-trade monitoring cycle.
- Live confidence components: Trend, Structure, Liquidity and Momentum.
- Trade Health states: HEALTHY, STABLE, WEAKENING and AT RISK.
- Deterministic AI Commentary based on live price action and trade state.
- Smart Alerts for meaningful confidence, health, risk and MFE-giveback changes.
- Historical Probability Engine embedded into live trade updates.
- No invented historical probabilities: empty samples remain explicitly in learning mode.
- Intelligence snapshots persisted in the database and shown in Journal / Trade Replay.

This is Phase 1 of v6.0. Later phases will expand similarity weighting, probability calibration and personalized intelligence.
