# LiquidityVisionBot v9.8.2 — Multi-Exchange Foundation

This release adds Bybit V5 as the second read-only exchange adapter and makes exchange health independent and explicit.

## Commands

- `/exchanges`
- `/exchange_symbol [bybit|binance] BTCUSDT`
- `/exchange_balance [bybit|binance]`
- `/exchange_positions [bybit|binance]`
- `/exchange_orders [bybit|binance] [SYMBOL]`

No order submission, modification, or cancellation method exists in this release.
